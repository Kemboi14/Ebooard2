Voting Types Guide

Simple Majority

  • • More than 50% of votes cast
  • • Minimum: 1 vote
  • • Best for: Routine decisions

Qualified Majority

  • • More than 60% of votes cast
  • • Minimum: 2 votes
  • • Best for: Important decisions

Two-Thirds Majority

  • • More than 66.67% of votes cast
  • • Minimum: 3 votes
  • • Best for: Constitutional changes

Unanimous

  • • 100% yes votes, no no votes
  • • Minimum: All eligible voters
  • • Best for: Critical decisions

Consensus

  • • No no votes, max 10% abstain
  • • Minimum: All eligible voters
  • • Best for: Collaborative decisions
